Tooltip

Small contextual labels that appear on hover or focus relative to a trigger element. Use tooltips to provide brief, supplementary information.

Accessibility

Add role="tooltip" on the tooltip content element and link it to the trigger with aria-describedby. Tooltips must be dismissible with Escape and should appear on both hover and focus.

Positions

The tooltip content appears on the specified side of the trigger. The arrow (via ::before) points toward the trigger element.
