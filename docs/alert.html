Alert

Alerts communicate important messages to users. Available in 4 types (success, warning, error, info) with default and subtle backgrounds.

Default

Success
Your changes have been saved successfully.
Warning
Your session will expire in 5 minutes. Please save your work.
Error
Failed to process your request. Please try again later.
Info
A new version of the application is available.

Subtle

Success (subtle)
Your profile has been updated.
Warning (subtle)
Storage is almost full. Consider upgrading your plan.
Error (subtle)
Unable to connect to the server.
Info (subtle)
Maintenance is scheduled for this weekend.
