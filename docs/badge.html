Accessibility

Accessibility Guidelines

Badges are primarily visual indicators. Do not rely on color alone to convey meaning -- always include descriptive text inside the badge. If a badge conveys status that is important for the user, ensure it is part of the accessible text content (not just an icon). All color combinations meet WCAG contrast requirements.
