Foundations

Design tokens are the building blocks of the design system. Foundation tokens hold raw values; semantic tokens add meaning and enable theming.

Colors

10 primitive color palettes with 11 shades each.

Theme Colors

Semantic color tokens for background, text, border, and feedback.

Typography

14 font sizes, 24 text styles, weights, line-heights.

Spacing

20 foundation spacing tokens plus semantic spacing.

Radius

8 border-radius tokens from none to full.

Elevation

5 shadow levels from none to xl.

Borders

Border widths and semantic border color tokens.

Motion

3 durations + 4 easings for transitions and animations.

Opacity

7 opacity levels for disabled states, overlays, and loading.

Z-index

6 stacking levels from base content to toast notifications.
